Introduction: Computer Architecture

By Quinn & Gianni - September 3, 2025 - 1 min

Every click, tap, and swipe hides a story. Beneath the apps, screens, and machines you use every day lies a hidden world of circuits, instructions, and clever engineering. This series takes you on a bottom-up journey from bits and bytes, through memory, storage, and CPUs, to the input and output devices that bridge the digital and physical worlds. By the end, computers won’t feel like mysterious black boxes, but like beautifully crafted, logical machines.
